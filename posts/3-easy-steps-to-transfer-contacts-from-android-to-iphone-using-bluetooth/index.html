Are you switching from an Android phone to an iPhone and don’t know how to transfer all your contacts? Step 1: First, turn on Bluetooth on both devices. To do this, simply open the Settings app on both devices and look for the Bluetooth option. Make sure to select the same Bluetooth network so that both phones can recognize each other.

Step 2: Next, open the Contacts app on the Android device and select the contacts that you would like to transfer. Tap the “Share” option and select “Bluetooth” from the share options. Your phone will then search for nearby devices.

Step 3: Once the iPhone device appears on the list of Bluetooth devices, select it and confirm that you want to transfer the contacts. The contacts will then be transferred to the iPhone and you can view them in the Contacts app.
