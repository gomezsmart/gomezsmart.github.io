2. Steps to Enable Screen Recording on iPhone 13 Pro Max#

Fortunately, the iPhone 13 Pro Max allows users to easily enable screen recording capabilities on their device. Here is a quick guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Start by accessing your device’s Control Center. To do this, press and hold the power button and volume up button concurrently until the Control Center appears.

Step 2: Next, tap the Screen Recording icon. It appears as a black circle with a white line running along the middle.

Step 3: A three-second timer will appear once the Screen Recording icon has been tapped. You can tap the Microphone icon to mute audio before the timer runs out.

Step 4: Once the timer has run out, your iPhone will begin to record your screen. To stop the recording, press the power and volume down buttons at the same time. The recording will automatically save to your device’s library and you’re all finished.
