3. Perform Manual Transfer#

When you decide to switch from your Android device to an iOS device, it is important to take some steps in order to properly transfer your data before you make the switch. One of the key elements of a successful transfer is transferring your WhatsApp messages between devices. Here is how you can manually transfer WhatsApp messages from Android to iPhone without using any third-party apps.

The first step is to create a backup for your WhatsApp messages. This can be done by going to WhatsApp Settings > Chats > Chat Backup and selecting the “Backup” option. Once this is done, the backup should be stored on your Android device.

The next step is to transfer the backup from your Android device to your iOS device. To do this, you can either use a USB cable between the devices or use a cloud storage system such as Google Drive or iCloud. Once the transfer is complete, you should be able to find the backup on the iOS device.

The last step is to restore the backup onto the iOS device by going to WhatsApp Settings > Chats > Chat Backup and selecting the “Restore” option. This will replace all existing WhatsApp messages on the device with the ones from the backup. Once the restore is complete, your WhatsApp messages should now be transferred from Android to iPhone.
