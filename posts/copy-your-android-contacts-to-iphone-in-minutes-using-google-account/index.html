Transferring contacts between mobile devices is often a big task. It often requires a third party app or manually transferring information. Fortunately, Google makes it easy to transfer your Android contacts to your new iPhone in minutes. All you need is a Google account and you can begin transferring data securely from one device to another. Here’s how.

Firstly, you must open Google Contacts from your Android device and sign in to the same Google account that you intend to use on the iPhone. Then, go to the Settings icon, and select the “Export” option. A pop-up window will appear, asking you if you want to export contacts to a file. Select “Yes” and the file will be automatically saved onto your device.

Afterwards, open your iPhone and go to the App Store. Search for “Google Contacts” and download the app. Once installed, open the app from the home screen and sign in using the same Google account you used on your Android device. The app will automatically find the file you saved and will give you an option to download it. Select “Yes” and your contacts will begin syncing.

Finally, you should now be able to find your contacts in the contacts list of your iPhone. To ensure that your data is synchronized across devices, make sure to keep the Google contacts app up to date. This will ensure that any changes made to contact information will remain updated across devices. With these few steps, you can easily transfer your Android contacts to your iPhone in minutes.

2. Transfer contacts from Android to iPhone using Google Account#

Transferring contacts from an Android smartphone to an iPhone can be a challenging task. However, with the help of your Google account, it can be an easy process. All you need to do is sign into your Google account on both devices, and your contacts will be synced automatically. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to transfer contacts from Android to iPhone using your Google account.

First, make sure that you are logged into the same Google account on both devices. Then, look for the Settings option from the main menu, and select Accounts & Sync/Google. On your Android device, check the box next to Sync Contacts and make sure the sync is turned on. Doing this will automatically sync your contacts to your Google account.

Next, log into the same Google account on your iPhone. From the Settings menu, look for Accounts & Passwords > Add Account > Google. Here you will be asked to enter your Google account’s credentials, enter them and tap Next. Lastly, make sure to enable Contacts sync from the Account Settings page.

After following the above steps, you should be all set. Your contacts should now appear on your iPhone, without you having to manually transfer them one by one. You can easily keep track of your contacts, whether you are using an Android or iPhone.

2. Steps to Transfer Contacts from an Android Device to an iPhone Using Google Account#

Transferring contacts from your android device to an iPhone can be a daunting task. However, it is possible to do this quickly and easily in just a few minutes using a Google account. Here are the steps to get it done:

  1. Sign into your Google account on your Android device.
  2. Select the contacts you want to transfer to the iPhone.
  3. Tap More -> Export -> Export to .vcf file.
  4. Go to the new iPhone and sign in with the same Google account.
  5. In settings, select Accounts & Passwords and then Add Account.
  6. Select Google and enter your Google account credentials then tap “Next”.
  7. Your contacts will appear. Tap “Save” in the top right corner to sync them with the iPhone.

That’s it, you are now ready to go. With a few simple steps you now have all of your android contacts on your iPhone.
