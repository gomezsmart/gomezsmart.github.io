2. Steps for Taking Partial Screenshots on Dell Chromebooks#

Taking partial screenshots on Dell Chromebooks is a fast and efficient way to save specific portions of an image or document. Here are the steps to help you get started.

First, click on the window you’d like to screenshot. Then, using two fingers, click and drag the area you want to capture. Once you’ve selected the area, press the Ctrl + Shift + window switcher keys on your keyboard. You should now have your partial screenshot appearing on your screen.

If you want to save the partial screenshot, open the Files application, navigate to Downloads, and click the Save button. You can name the image as you wish. To open the partial screenshot, click on the image, then click the Open With button and select an image editor, such as the Paint app.

To quickly share the partial screenshot, right-click the image and select the Copy to Clipboard option. This will save the screenshot on your clipboard, from which you can paste it onto any other application or website that accepts images.

Finally, if you want to delete the partial screenshot, go to the Files app, look for the screenshot in the Downloads folder, and click the Trash icon to delete it.

With these few simple steps, you can easily take partial screenshots on your Dell Chromebook.
