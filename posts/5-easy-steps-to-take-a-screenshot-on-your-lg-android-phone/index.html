Q1: What is a screenshot?

A1: A screenshot is an image that captures what is currently displayed on a smartphone or computer screen. It can be used to show others what you see on your device or to save something you see temporarily.

Q2: How do I take a screenshot on my LG Android phone?

A2: Taking a screenshot on an LG Android phone is simple and requires just a few steps. First, locate the Power and Volume Down buttons. A4: LG Android phones typically save screenshots in the popular Portable Network Graphics (PNG) format.
