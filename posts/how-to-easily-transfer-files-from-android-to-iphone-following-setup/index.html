2. Transfer Files#

When you have recently purchased a new iPhone, you may be wondering how you can transfer the contents of your Android device, including contacts, photos, videos, and other data, to your new iPhone. Fortunately, there are a few easy steps to transfer files from Android to iPhone after setup.

First, connect your old Android device and new iPhone to the same Wi-fi network. Then, locate and open the “Move to iOS” app on your Android device. This app will help you securely transfer data from your Android device to your new iPhone.

Once the app is installed, follow the instructions provided and accept the terms and conditions to transfer files. Depending on the amount of data you are transferring, it may take some time for the data to transfer over. All of the content that you select to be transferred will be sent to the new iPhone.

After the transfer is complete, you can disconnect the devices. Your new iPhone should now hold all of the content from your old Android. Congratulations! Now, you have successfully learned how to transfer files from Android to iPhone after setup.

3. Finalizing Setup#

After setting up your new iPhone, you may want to transfer files from your old Android phone. Doing so can help you keep all your important photos, documents and other media in one place. Here’s how to transfer files from Android to iPhone after setup. Firstly, check if your Android phone supports Wi-Fi direct. If it does, you can connect both phones to the same network, and then use a file sharing app such as SHAREit or Xender to move the files. Alternatively, if your Android phone supports a USB OTG, you can connect it to the iPhone using an adapter. Once connected, open the Documents folder on your iPhone and select ‘import from Android’. You may also be able to use a cloud storage platform to transfer files from your Android to your iPhone. Install the same cloud services on both phones, and then upload the files you want to move to the cloud. Lastly, if none of the above methods work, you can transfer the files manually. You’ll need to connect your Android to a computer, copy the files you want to transfer, and then connect your iPhone to the same computer. After that, paste the files into the Documents folder on your iPhone.

2. Connecting Devices#

Connecting Android and iPhone devices has become easier than ever with the introduction of Setup. This two-step process makes transferring everything from images and videos to music and documents a breeze. To get started, here is how to easily transfer files from Android to iPhone using Setup.

First, open the Setup app on both devices and select the ‘Transfer from Android’ option. Then, select the data you would like to transfer, such as photos, videos, and music. Once you have chosen the data you want to transfer, confirm the selections and click ‘Next’.

On the Android device, enter the pairing code that appears on the iPhone device. Then, select the data types you want to transfer, such as photos, videos, music, documents, and contacts. Once the selections are made, tap ‘Transfer’. This will transfer the selected data to the iPhone.

Finally, after the transfer is complete, on the Android device, select ‘Done’ and confirm the successful completion of the transfer. On the iPhone device, the transferred data will be saved in the appropriate location. With Setup, transferring files from Android to iPhone devices has become easier and faster.
