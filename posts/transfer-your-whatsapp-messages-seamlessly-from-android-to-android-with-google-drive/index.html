1. How to Set Up Google Drive for WhatsApp Message Backup#

Backup your WhatsApp messages to the cloud with ease by using Google Drive with these simple steps. First, open the WhatsApp application on your Android device, then go to ‘Settings’ and select ‘Chats’ and then ‘Chat Backup.’ Here you can select when you want to back up your chats to Google Drive as well as choose the frequency you want the back-up to occur. Next, tap the ‘Back up’ button. When the back-up process has been completed, it will appear in the list of your backups in Google Drive. To transfer the messages to a new Android phone, install WhatsApp and sign in with the same Google account on the new device. When you restore your chat messages to the new device, you’ll be prompted to enter a backup name. Here you can enter the name of the backed-up chats and then tap ‘Restore.’ To finish up, tap the ‘Next’ button and your WhatsApp messages will be transferred from one device to another.

Finally, tap on “Restore” when prompted to do so. This will restore all of your WhatsApp messages from your Google Drive backup to your new device. Keep in mind that this process may take several minutes to complete, depending on the number of messages in your chat history.
