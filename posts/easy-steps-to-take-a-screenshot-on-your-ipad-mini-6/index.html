- Connecting Your Printer#

The iPad Mini 6 is a great device to take with you on the go. But how do you know how to take a print screen if you’re new to the device? This tutorial guide will show you how to do it.

First, you will need to open the Control Center. To do that, just swipe down from the top right corner of the iPad’s display. Once the Control Center is open, you will see a number of options at the bottom. Tap the More button and then tap the Print Screen button.

The next step is to select the area on the screen that you want to capture. Tap and drag with your finger from the top left corner of the area you want to capture and drag it across to the bottom right. The area you have chosen will be highlighted in blue.

When you’ve finished selecting the area, tap the Capture button at the bottom of the screen. This will take a print screen and save it to your Photos. You can view, edit and share your capture to any of your devices or social media platforms.

3. Saving Your Screenshot#

Taking a screenshot on your iPad Mini 6 is simpler than you think. Here are a few easy steps to get you started:

Firstly, locate the ‘Home’ and ‘Sleep/Wake’ buttons on the top of your iPad Mini 6. Next, gently press and hold down both buttons simultaneously. Once you hear a shutter sound, release both buttons. Your iPad Mini 6 will temporarily create a screenshot of what is currently being displayed on the screen.

Go to the ‘Photos’ app on your iPad Mini 6 and click on ‘Albums’. Your screenshot will be saved under the folder ‘Screenshots’. You can also access the screenshot from this folder via the thumbnail view. Just tap and hold the desired screenshot to edit, share or delete.

To share your screenshot, tap on the arrow icon located on the top right corner of your screen. Select the sharing method you would like to use and add the necessary details if needed.

Lastly, if you want to keep the screenshot as a memory, you can save it into your Camera Roll. Tap on the ‘Share’ button followed by the ‘Save Image’ button. Your screenshot will now be stored in the Camera Roll folder.

1. Taking a Screenshot#

If you want to edit the screenshot before sharing or printing, tap the Edit icon located at the top of the screen. Here, you can crop or rotate the image, as well as add filters and text. When you are done, tap Done and you will be prompted to save or share the edited screenshot.

Q1. What are the steps for taking a screenshot on an iPad Mini 6?

A1. Taking a screenshot on an iPad Mini 6 is easy and can be done with the following steps:

  1. Press and hold the Sleep/Wake button and the Home button at the same time.
  2. When the screenshot has been taken, you will hear a shutter sound and will see a preview of the screenshot in the bottom left corner of the screen.
  3. You can share or edit the screenshot with the tools that appear in the preview or head to the Photos app to view and share it.

Q2. Can I take a screenshot on an iPad Mini 6 with a mouse?

A2. No, you cannot take a screenshot on an iPad Mini 6 with a mouse. You must use the Sleep/Wake and Home button combination to take a screenshot.

Q3. Where do I find the screenshots I’ve taken on my iPad Mini 6?

A3. Screenshots taken on an iPad Mini 6 are stored in the Photos app. To view and share them, open the Photos app and select the Screenshots folder in the Albums tab.

Q4. What type of file is a screenshot on an iPad Mini 6?

A4. Screenshots taken on an iPad Mini 6 are stored in .PNG format.

Q5. Can I take a screenshot of a video on an iPad Mini 6?

A5. Yes, you can take a screenshot of a video on an iPad Mini 6. Simply press and hold the Sleep/Wake button and the Home button at the same time while the video is playing and you will be able to take a screenshot.
