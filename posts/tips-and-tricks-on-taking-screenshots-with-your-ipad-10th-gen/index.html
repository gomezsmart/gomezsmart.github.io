To take a screenshot on your iPad 10th Generation device, press and hold the Sleep/Wake button and the Home button at the same time. This will capture an image of the Home screen and any open apps, and the screenshot will appear in the lower left-hand corner of the screen. Tap this to open the preview.

You can then either save the screenshot to your iPad’s Photos app by tapping the Share icon, or you can edit it using the Markup tool. This tool allows you to add text, draw on the image, and even crop it. When you’re done, you can share the screenshot with friends, family or colleagues.

If you don’t want to save the screenshot to your Photos app, you can also tap the Done button in the top-right corner of the preview. This will save the screenshot to the clipboard, and you can paste it into any app of your choosing.

With the screenshot feature on the iPad 10th Generation device, you can quickly and easily capture any moment you want. Whether you want to save it on your device or share it with someone else, it’s an easy and convenient way to capture and store your memories.
