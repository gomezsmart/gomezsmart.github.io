2. Taking Screenshots on Samsung Laptops#

Taking screenshots on a Samsung laptop is easy and straightforward. You can take a screenshot of your entire screen or just a part of the screen. Here are the steps to help you take a screenshot on a Samsung laptop:

  1. To take a full screenshot, simply press the “Print Screen” (PrtSc) button on your keyboard. The image will be saved in the clipboard.

  2. If you want to take a partial screenshot, press “Alt” + “Print Screen”. This will take a screenshot of just the active window.

  3. To save the screenshot to your computer, open the “Paint” application and press “Ctrl” + “V” to paste the screenshot. Then, click “File” and select “Save As”.

  4. Finally, name your file and choose the file type you want to save it as. When you’re finished, click “Enter” and save your screenshot.

That’s it! Taking screenshots on a Samsung laptop is easy, and now you know how to do it.
