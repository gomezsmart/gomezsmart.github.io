1. Prepare Your iPhone and Android Phone for WhatsApp Data Transfer#

If you want to switch from an iPhone to an Android phone and keep your WhatsApp messages, you need to transfer them first. The process may seem daunting, but it’s actually easier than you think. Here’s how you can transfer your WhatsApp messages from your iPhone to an Android phone without using a cable.

To prepare your device for a smooth and successful transfer, make sure your iPhone and Android are running the latest system updates. This will ensure both phones can be properly connected via the same Wi-Fi network. Additionally, keep both devices connected to power sources until the transfer is complete.

Next, you need to back up your WhatsApp data from your iPhone. To do this, open the WhatsApp app, go to Settings, Account, and Backup. Select “Back Up Now” to create a full backup of your WhatsApp conversations and media. Make sure your device has enough free space to back up everything.

After that, you can now download and install WhatsApp on your new Android phone. Once the installation is complete, open WhatsApp, and select “Restore” when prompted. This will prompt your Android device to restore the data from your previous WhatsApp backup from the iPhone.

Finally, all your WhatsApp messages, media, and other conversation data should be now transferred to your new Android device. You can start chatting on your Android device just like you did on the iPhone.
