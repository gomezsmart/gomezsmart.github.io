Taking screenshots on the iPad Mini 6 is easier than you think. With just a few button presses, you can capture whatever is on your screen. Here’s a step-by-step guide to taking screenshots on the iPad Mini 6:

Step 1: Press the Volume up button and the Lock/Power button at the same time. You’ll hear a camera shutter sound and see a white border blink around the edge of the screen.

Step 2: Open the Photos app to find the screenshot. It will be the first image in the All Photos album.

Step 3: Open the screenshot in the Photos app to edit it. You can use the tools on the top of the screen to crop and rotate it.

Step 4: When you’re happy with the screenshot, tap the Done button to save it. If you don’t tap Done, the screenshot won’t be saved.
