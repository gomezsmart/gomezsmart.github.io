Step 2: Pair the devices. Once you have enabled Bluetooth in both devices, you will need to pair them. To do this, tap the Bluetooth icon on the first device, and then select the second device from the list.

Step 3: Select the data you want to transfer. Once the devices have been paired, you can select the files you want to transfer. To do this, open the File Manager on the first device, and select the files you want to transfer.

Step 4: Initiate the transfer. Once you have selected the files you want to transfer, you can initiate the transfer. To do this, tap on the Bluetooth icon on the first device, and then select the second device.

Step 5: Wait for the transfer to finish. Once the transfer has been initiated, you can wait for the transfer to complete. Depending on the size of the transfer, this could take anywhere from a few minutes to a few hours.
