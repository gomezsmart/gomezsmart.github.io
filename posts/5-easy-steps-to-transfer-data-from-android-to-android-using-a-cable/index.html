Do you want to know how to transfer data from your old android device to your new android device easily in just 5 steps? Whether you’re transferring important documents, your favorite music, or the photos from your last vacation, the process doesn’t have to be complicated. With these 5 easy steps, you can have your data safely transferred from one Android device to another in no time – all using just a cable!

Step one requires you to plug the USB cable into the source device, then the other end of the cable into the receiving device. Make sure that both devices are unlocked in order for the transfer to begin.

The second step is to navigate to the “Settings” menu on both devices so you can select the “Developer Options”. After selecting this option, scroll down and turn on the “Enable USB Debugging” option to begin the transfer.

Step three requires you to select the type of data you would like to transfer. To do this, on the source device, select the “Data Transfer” option from the menu and then choose the type of files you’d like to transfer.

The fourth step is to select the exact data you’d like to transfer. On the source device, you’ll have the option to select which files you would like to include in the transfer. Make sure to select only the items that you’d like to transfer before continuing.

Finally, the fifth and final step is for the transfer to begin. On both devices, you’ll be prompted to confirm the transfer, simply select “Yes” and the files and data will begin to transfer from the source device to the receiving device.
