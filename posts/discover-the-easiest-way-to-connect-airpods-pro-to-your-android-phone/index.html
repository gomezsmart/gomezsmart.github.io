II. Additional Troubleshooting Tips#

When it comes to connecting your AirPods Pro to your Android phone, it’s important to know the right steps. This guide will show you exactly how to do it, as well as provide some additional troubleshooting tips.

To connect your AirPods Pro to your Android phone, press and hold the setup button on the back of the charging case for about two seconds. Once you get the connection pop-up on your phone, just tap on the AirPods Pro to select it and the connection will be established.

If for some reason your AirPods Pro don’t connect to your phone, try resetting the settings by pressing and holding the setup button for at least 15 seconds. This will reset the connection.

If it still doesn’t work, try connecting your AirPods Pro to another device like a laptop or tablet. If they connect on a different device, then the issue may be with your phone’s Bluetooth connection. Try restarting your phone or checking for a Bluetooth firmware update.

Finally, if the AirPods Pro still don’t work, make sure the charging case is charged. Low battery can be the cause of connection issues. Charge the case for a few minutes, and then try connecting again.
