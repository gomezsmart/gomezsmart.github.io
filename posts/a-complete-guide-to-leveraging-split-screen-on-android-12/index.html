Firstly, open two apps you wish to view side-by-side. Secondly, press and hold the recent apps button. This will open the Overview menu. You can now drag and drop one of the apps to the top of the Overview menu, and the other app to the bottom. This will enable the Split Screen feature and the two apps will be visible side-by-side.

If you wish to resize the two apps, bring your finger to the middle of the two apps and drag it left or right. The apps will automatically adjust their sizes accordingly. Q1 : What is split screen?

A1 : Split screen is an Android 12 feature that lets users multitask between two apps by dividing the screen into two separate windows. This allows users to perform tasks side-by-side without having to switch back and forth between apps.

Q2 : How do I access split screen on Android 12?

A2 : To access split screen on Android 12, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app you want to use in split screen by tapping on its icon.

  2. Once the app is open, tap and hold on the recent apps button on the bottom right corner of the screen.

  3. Select the “Split Screen” option from the menu that appears.

  4. The chosen app will now be shown in the top half of the screen and a selection bar will appear at the bottom.

  5. Select the second app you want to use in the split screen from the selection bar.

Q3 : Can I resize the split screen windows?

A3 : Yes, you can resize the split screen windows to better suit your needs. To do so, simply press and hold on the black bar between the windows, then drag it left or right to adjust the size of each window.

Q4 : How do I exit split screen mode?

A4 : To exit split screen mode, press and hold on the recent apps button at the bottom right corner of the screen. Then, select the “Close Split Screen” option from the menu that appears.

Q5 : Are there any apps that don’t support split screen?

A5 : Yes, some apps do not support split screen, so they will not appear in the selection bar when trying to open them in split screen. To check if an app supports split screen, look for the “Split Screen” option in its in-app settings.
