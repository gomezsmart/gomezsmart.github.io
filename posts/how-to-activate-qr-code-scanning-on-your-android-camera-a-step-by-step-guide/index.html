1. Enable QR Code Scanning On Your Android Camera#

Enabling QR Code Scanning on your Android Camera is a simple process. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

  1. Open the Camera app on your Android device.
  2. Tap on the Settings button at the top-right of the Camera app.
  3. Scroll down and tap on ‘Scan QR Code’ and toggle it to ‘On.’
  4. That’s it, your Android Camera app is now enabled to scan QR Codes.

With your Android Camera app now capable of scanning QR Codes, you’ll be able to quickly access a variety of websites and information without the need to type long URLs. This feature saves you time and effort, making your life easier.
