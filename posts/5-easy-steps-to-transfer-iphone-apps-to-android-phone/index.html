5. Restore the Data on the Android Apps#

Transferring iOS apps to Android phones is a seamless process. As with any transfer, there are five steps to follow to make sure the transition of data goes smoothly. Here is a simple five-step guide to help you transfer your data from one device to the other.

Step 1: Before you begin, make sure that both your iPhone and Android devices have the latest updates. Also, you need to make sure you have your iCloud data backed up.

Step 2: Open the app you want to transfer to your Android device. Tap the “Settings” tab. Then tap the “Data and Privacy” button. You will see an option to “Export Your Data”. Tap the Export Your Data button to generate an export file.

Step 3: Download the file generated from Step 2 onto your computer. You can then transfer the file to your Android phone via USB or Bluetooth.

Step 4: Once the data is on your Android device, open the app you want to transfer the data to. In the Settings tab, navigate to the “Data and Privacy” page and tap the “Import Your Data” button.

Step 5: Select the file you transferred to your Android device. This will restore the data from your iPhone to your Android device.

That’s it! Now you have successfully transferred the data from your iPhone to your Android device. Following these easy steps, you can transfer any app’s data from one device to another with no issue.
