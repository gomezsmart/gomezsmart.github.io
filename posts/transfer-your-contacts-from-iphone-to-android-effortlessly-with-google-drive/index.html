1. How to Move Contacts from iPhone to Android Using Google Drive#

Transferring contacts from an iPhone to an Android device can sometimes be a difficult process. Fortunately, there is an easier way to move contacts from an iPhone to an Android device with Google Drive. By following these steps, you can transfer your contacts from iPhone to Android device easily and quickly.

First, open the Google Drive app and make sure you are signed in to your Google Account. Then, open the Settings of your iPhone and select Mail, Contacts, Calendars. Select Import SIM Contacts and tap Copy to Google Drive. After the copying process is done, open the Google Drive app and tap the menu option at the top left corner. Select the contacts you exported from iPhone and tap the download button at the top of the screen.

On your Android device, open the Google Drive app and sign in to your Google Account. Select My Drive and open the contacts you downloaded earlier. Finally, tap the More option and select Export to device. Select the exact contact list you want to import and confirm the transfer. All the contacts from your iPhone will now be moved to your Android device.

Using Google Drive is one of the simplest ways to move contacts between different phones. By following these steps, you can easily transfer contacts from iPhone to Android without any hassle.

i. Setting up Google Drive on iPhone#

In this article, we will explain how to move contacts from iPhone to Android using Google Drive. Google Drive is a cloud storage service that offers secure storage of files from all your devices, including mobile phones. Using Google Drive, you can easily move your contacts from your iPhone to your Android device. Here are the steps for transferring contacts using Google Drive:

First, open the Google Drive app on your iPhone. Select the “More” tab and then tap “Settings.” Under the “Accounts and Sync” section, select “Add Account.” Choose the “Google” option and sign in using your Google account.

Once you have signed in, select the “Contacts” tab and tap “Sync Now.” This will begin the process of transferring your contacts from your iPhone to your Google account.

After the synchronization is complete, open the Google Drive app on your Android device. Select “More” and then tap “Settings.” Under “Accounts and Sync,” select “Add Account.” Choose the “Google” option and sign in using your Google account.

Once you have signed in, select the “Contacts” tab and tap “Sync Now.” This will begin the process of transferring your contacts from your Google account to your Android device. Once the synchronization is complete, your contacts will be available on your Android device.

By following these steps, you can easily move your contacts from iPhone to Android using Google Drive. Transferring your contacts to Google Drive is a fast and secure way to keep all of your contacts in one place.

ii. Transferring Contacts to Android Device#

Moving contacts from one device to the other can be a tricky process. Luckily, using Google Drive is a great way to transfer all your contacts from your iPhone to your Android device. In this tutorial, we will go through the steps to make this process as painless as possible.

The first thing to do is to open the Contacts app on your iPhone and tap “Groups” in the upper-right corner. Select “All iCloud” and make sure that all of your contacts are checked. Next, tap the “Share” icon in the upper-right corner and select “Export vCard”. This will save your contacts as a .vcf file in your iCloud Drive.

Once you have exported your contacts to your iCloud Drive, open the Google Drive app on your Android device and tap the “Plus” icon in the bottom-right corner. From here, select “Upload” and select the .vcf file you just exported from your iPhone. The file will now be uploaded to your Google Drive.

The next step is to open the Contacts app on your Android device and tap the three-dot menu icon in the upper-right corner. From here, select “Import” and select “From Google Drive”. This will open the Google Drive app and you can select the .vcf file you just uploaded from your iPhone. Once you select the file, your contacts will be imported to your Android device.

Now your contacts are successfully transferred from your iPhone to your Android device using Google Drive! This process is simple and quick, so you won’t have to worry about losing any of your contacts in the transition.
