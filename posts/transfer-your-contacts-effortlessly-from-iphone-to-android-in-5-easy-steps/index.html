3. Step 3: Transfer Contacts to Your Android Phone#

Step 3 is all about getting your contacts onto your Android phone. To do this, you’ll need to use a tool like Google Drive. First, open the Google Drive app on your iPhone and sign in to your Google account. Once you’ve logged in, type Contacts into the search bar. Select the Contacts app and hit Transfer. This will ensure that all of your contacts are synced to your Google Drive account. Finally, open the Contacts app on your Android phone, sign into the same Google account, and select Sync Contacts. Now, all of your contacts will be transferred from your iPhone to your Android device.
