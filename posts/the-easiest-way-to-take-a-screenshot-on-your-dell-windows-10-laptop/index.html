To take a screenshot on your Dell Windows 10 laptop, the simplest way is through Print Screen (abbreviated to PrntScr). This will capture a screenshot of the entire screen and save it to your computer’s clipboard. To access the screenshot, open a graphic editor, such as Microsoft Paint, and paste the screenshot into the editor.

If you wish to take a screenshot of just a single window, you can do so by pressing the Alt and PrntScr keys at the same time. This will capture a screenshot of a selected window and save it to your computer’s clipboard. Again, you can access the screenshot by pasting it into a graphic editor.

You can also take screenshots using the Windows 10 ‘Snipping Tool’. This is a useful tool for taking screenshots of specific regions of your computer’s screen. Simply open the ‘Snipping Tool’ and select the ‘New’ option. You will then be able to select the region of the screen that you wish to capture.

Finally, the Windows 10 ‘Game Bar’ feature also allows you to take screenshots of games. To use this, open the Game Bar, press ‘Windows + G’, and then press ‘Camera’ to capture the screenshot.

As you can see, Dell Windows 10 laptops offer a range of different ways to take screenshots. Whether you are capturing an image of a single window or a specific region of your screen, you can find the screenshot feature that meets your needs.
