2. Steps to Take a Screenshot on Your MacBook Air M2#

Taking screenshots is an easy and intuitive process on a MacBook Air M2. Whether you want to capture an entire webpage or just a portion, you can take a high-quality screenshot with the right tools. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to take a screenshot on your MacBook Air M2:

  1. To capture the entire screen, press the “Command + Shift + 3” keys all together. This will take a screenshot of the whole screen and save it as a .png image to the desktop.

  2. To capture a portion of the screen, press the “Command + Shift + 4” keys all together. This will bring up a crosshair cursor, which you can use to select the area that you want to take a screenshot of.

  3. Press the spacebar to switch to the camera icon, and you can then click to take a screenshot of a specific window. The .png image of the window will be saved to the desktop.

  4. To save a screenshot to the clipboard instead of to the desktop, add the Ctrl key to either of the two commands above. For example, press “Command + Shift + Ctrl + 3” to take a screenshot of the entire screen and save it to the clipboard.
