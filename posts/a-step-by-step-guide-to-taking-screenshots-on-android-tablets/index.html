Capturing screenshots on an Android tablet is an easy and useful task. A white border should appear for a few seconds, confirming that the screenshot has been taken. Usually, the screenshot can be found in a folder named Screenshots in the tablet’s gallery. Alternatively, there are a few apps such as Screenshot Touch, which can be used to capture screenshots as well. The app works by tapping on the screenshot button that appears in the notification menu. After taking a screenshot, the image can be edited, cropped, or shared. With these steps, taking screenshots on an Android tablet is a simple and straightforward task.

Additionally, you can also quickly and easily take scrolling screenshots, which come in handy when dealing with long web pages.
