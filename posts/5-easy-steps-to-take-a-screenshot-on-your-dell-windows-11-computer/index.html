- Step 1: Open the Window of the Content You Want to Take a Screenshot of#

Step 1: Open the Window of the Content You Want to Take a Screenshot of. To take a screenshot of your Dell Windows 11 computer, first open the window of the content you want to take a screenshot of. Step 3: Open the Paint Program. To paste the screenshot, you will need to open the Paint program. To do this, open the Start menu, search for Paint, and select the Paint program from the search results.

Step 4: Paste and Save the Image. Once you have opened the Paint program, press Ctrl + V to paste the screenshot. From here, you can edit the image if you desire. When you are done editing, click the Save button to save the image.
