2. Setting Up Automation on Android#

Android devices have the capability to add applications to the Home Screen automatically. This feature allows the user to customize their device to better suit their preferences. To do this, one needs to enable the Android Automation feature. To enable Automation, one has to go to their device Settings by swiping down from the top of the screen. Once in the Settings page, one has to select the Accessibility option and then choose the Automation settings. From there, one can enable the feature by pushing the toggle switch and selecting which applications to add to the Home Screen. After that, the applications will be added to the Home Screen automatically. To customize your Home Screen further, one can change the layout of the apps by selecting the ‘Change Layout’ option in the Automation settings. This will allow you to organize your apps in a neat and organized way that best suits you.
