2. Clearing Cache and Cookies in Chrome Settings#

Clearing cache and cookies from your Android Chrome browser can be done easily through the device settings. To do this, open the Settings menu on your Android device and select Apps & notifications. Scroll down the list to locate Chrome and click on it. Tap Storage and then Clear Cache. This will delete the temporary data stored in the cache. Next, tap Clear Data and then confirm your action to delete all stored cookies. Restart Chrome to finish. Following these simple steps will help to keep your Android Chrome browser free from clutter.
