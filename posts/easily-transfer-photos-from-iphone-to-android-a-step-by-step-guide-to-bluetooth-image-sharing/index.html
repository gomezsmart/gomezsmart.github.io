1. How to Send Images from iPhone to Android Using Bluetooth#

This article will walk you through the steps necessary to transfer photos from an iPhone to an Android using Bluetooth. First, you will need to make sure that both devices have Bluetooth enabled. On the iPhone, you can go to Settings > Bluetooth, while on the Android look for a Bluetooth icon in the quick settings menu. Next, initiate the Bluetooth connection process by selecting the device you want to connect to from the list. Once the devices are paired, you will be able to send images from the iPhone to the Android with just a few taps.

To send photos, open the photo you want to send and select the share button from the bottom of the screen. Choose Send via Bluetooth from the list of options and select the device to complete the pairing. If the pairing was successful, the images will be sent from the iPhone to the Android device.

To receive the photo, open the Bluetooth pairing window on the Android device. If you paired the devices successfully, the iPhone will appear in the list of ‘Available devices’. Once the connection is established, the image will be downloaded and saved in the phone.

Finally, make sure to turn off the Bluetooth connection when you’re done. This will save battery and help maintain the security of the connection. With these easy steps, you can now quickly and easily transfer photos from an iPhone to an Android using Bluetooth.
