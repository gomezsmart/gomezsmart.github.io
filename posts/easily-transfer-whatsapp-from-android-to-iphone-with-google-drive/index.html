- Introduction of Google Drive#

Google Drive is an online storage service offered by Google. With Google Drive, you can easily transfer WhatsApp messages from Android to iPhone and also keep your chat history safe in the cloud. To transfer WhatsApp using Google Drive, first of all, you need to backup your chats on Android to Google Drive. Then, download and restore the backed-up files from Google Drive to your iPhone. This tutorial will guide you through the whole process.

At first, make sure your Android device is connected to a Wi-Fi network and also has enough storage space to perform the backup. Then, open WhatsApp and go to its Settings > Chats > Chat Backup. Tap ‘Back up’ to start creating the backup.

Once the backup is created, open the Google Drive app and log in with your Google account credentials. Tap the ‘Plus’ icon to create a new folder, name it and tap ‘OK’ to save. After that, tap the ‘Backup’ folder and the files will be uploaded and stored into that folder.

After the backup is synced with Google Drive, go to the iPhone you want to transfer the talks to and log in to the app with the same Google account credentials. Navigate to the ‘Backup’ folder and tap the files to begin downloading them. Once the files have been downloaded, open the WhatsApp app on the iPhone and tap ‘Restore chat history’ to restore the backup.

This is how you can easily transfer WhatsApp messages from Android to iPhone using Google Drive. It is a simple process that can help you keep your chat history safe, without needing to use any third-party apps.
